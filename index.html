M.anifest

GOD MC


Musician & Poet

Born Kwame Ametepee Tsikata, known professionally as M.anifest is the Ghanaian musician, rapper and record producer. He won Best Rapper and Hip-Hop song of the year at the 2017 Ghana Music Awards. He has worked with Damon Albarn, Flea, Tony Allen, Erykah Badu, and is featured on five songs on the Rocket Juice and The Moon album. He is the grandson of one of Africa's foremost ethnomusicologists and composers J.H. Kwabena Nketia.In 2012, The Strand on BBC Radio tipped him as one of four acts to look out for in 2012.
